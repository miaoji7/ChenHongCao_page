Biography

Chenhong Cao is currently lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the School of Computer Engineering and Science, Shanghai University, P.R.China. She received the PhD degree from the College of Computer Science and Technology in Zhejiang University in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Wei Dong. She received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in computer science from Northeastern University, China, in 2011 and 2013, respectively.
From 2018 to 2019, she worked in Alibaba-Zhejiang University Joint Research Institute of Frontier Technologies as a researcher.

Her research interests mainly focus on the network and system of the Internet of Things, network measurement and security, and wireless sensing. She received Shanghai Science and Technology Young Talents Sailing Program in 2020（上海市青年科技英才扬帆计划项目获得者，2020年）. She has managed 1 National Natural Science Foundation Project, and participated 2 National High-Tech Programs as a key researcher.

We are actively looking for self-motivated graduate candidates and undergraduate students, who would like to conduct research at the frontier of network and security, to join us. Welcome to send me an e-mail (caoch@shu.edu.cn) with your CV or drop by my office.
